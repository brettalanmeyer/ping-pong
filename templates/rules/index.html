Singles

A match is played best of 1, 2 of 3, or 3 of 5 games.

For each game, the first player to reach 21 points (or otherwise specified) wins that game, however a game must be won by at least a two point margin.

Doubles

A match is played best of 1, 2 of 3, or 3 of 5 games.

For each game, the first player to reach 21 points (or otherwise specified) wins that game, however a game must be won by at least a two point margin.

Singles/Doubles Serve Rules

If you throw the ball in the air, attempt to hit it, and fail, it is a loss of point.

ANY serve (including a net hit) that does not hit your side first, followed by you opponents side is a loss of point.

DOUBLES only rule: ALL serves must be hit from serving team's right square first, followed by the returning teams diagonal square. Any serve that fails to meet this condition (even a net serve) results in loss of point.
